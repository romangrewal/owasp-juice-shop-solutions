## Outdated Allowlist 
### Challenge: Let us redirect you to one of our crypto currency addresses which are not promoted any longer.

* Log in to the application as any user
* Order some items
* Click on "Your Basket"
* Checkout your items
* Add a new address if you have to
* Click "Continue"
* Select a delivery speed
* Click "Continue"
* On the "My Payment Options" screen, click "Other payment options" toggle
* Observe the "redirect?to=" variable when hovering over the Merchandise links
* Open the browser inspector
* Inspect sources to open main.js
* Ctrl-F to find "redirect?to=" variable
* Observe addresses of crypto wallets
* Visit one of the crypto wallets to complete the challenge
